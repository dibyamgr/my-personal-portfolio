# Audio Files for Music Player

## Lofi Background Music

To enable the music player functionality, add your lofi background music file here:

### File Requirements:
- **Filename**: `lofi-background.mp3`
- **Format**: MP3 (recommended) or other web-compatible audio formats
- **Size**: Keep under 5MB for optimal loading
- **Duration**: 2-5 minutes (will loop automatically)

### Where to Get Lofi Music:

1. **Free Sources (Royalty-free)**:
   - [Pixabay](https://pixabay.com/music/) - Free lofi tracks
   - [Bensound](https://www.bensound.com/) - Royalty-free music
   - [Free Music Archive](https://freemusicarchive.org/) - Various genres including lofi
   - [YouTube Audio Library](https://www.youtube.com/audiolibrary) - Free music for creators

2. **Paid/Premium**:
   - [Epidemic Sound](https://www.epidemicsound.com/)
   - [Artlist](https://artlist.io/)
   - [AudioJungle](https://audiojungle.net/)

### How to Add:

1. Download your chosen lofi track
2. Rename it to `lofi-background.mp3`
3. Place it in this directory: `/public/audio/`
4. The music player will automatically load it

### Notes:
- Make sure you have the rights/license to use the music
- The player includes play/pause controls
- Music will loop automatically
- Users can toggle it on/off with the floating music button
